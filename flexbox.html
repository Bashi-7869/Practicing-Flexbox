Pangong Tso Lake

If you’re confused while choosing the places to visit in Ladakh, Pangong Tso Lake should be the first one to get added to your list. The highest lake in India offers some breathtaking views to leave you spellbound.

Nubra Valley

The uniqueness of Nubra Valley will astonish you while you will be roaming across the green valley. Being one of the most transcending valleys in India, this valley is a perfect destination for photo sessions.

Khardung La Pass

Ladakh’ means land of high passes and it will be a great miss if you don’t explore the thrilling passes of Ladakh. plan a visit to Khardung La Pass on priority to witness the rigidity nature in this region.

Zanskar Valley

Having a trip to Zanskar Valley will take the delicacy of your trip to another level. The startling valley has a different ambiance that is bound to blow your mind. Trekkers will love trekking to high altitudes through the frozen Zanskar river.

Thiksey Monastery

What can be more exciting than discovering a 12-storied ancient monastery on the hilltop No need to specify that the mysterious essence of ancient Buddhism will help you get new perspectives.

Royal Leh Palace

Visiting this old palace will add some vibrant vibes to your trip.you’ll get glimpses of the lifestyles of the royal family of this palace.The building has historical significance and you can’t come to know about the special historical stories unless you arrange a guided trip.
